Mission

Through sustainable direct lending, technical assistance and community collaboration, Rocky Mountain Community Reinvestment Corporation (RMCRC) will facilitate the development and preservation of safe and clean affordable housing and community facilities that serve low-to moderate-income individuals, families and underserved communities throughout the Rocky Mountain region.

Vision

Working closely with our community partners, RMCRC seeks to eradicate the severe shortage of affordable housing that exists throughout the Rocky Mountain region.
